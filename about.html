How We Improve

Our feedback loop — how we never stop improving

Every piece of guest feedback we receive goes through a structured four-step process. We do not just read reviews — we act on them, close every gap, and verify the improvement.

01
Collect
Gather feedback via Google reviews, WhatsApp & in-salon conversations after every visit.
02
Analyse
Review every concern — positive and negative — weekly across all 5 locations.
03
Root Cause
Identify the exact cause of every service gap and assign a clear corrective action.
04
Implement
Train, upgrade and improve — then verify the fix so the same gap never recurs.
