Job Summary

We are seeking a skilled Female All-Rounder who can confidently handle multiple beauty services and deliver consistent, high-quality experiences to clients. This role is ideal for professionals who enjoy versatility and client interaction in a fast-paced salon environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform beauty services including facials, waxing, threading, clean-ups, and basic hair services
  • Provide manicure, pedicure, and basic spa services as per salon standards
  • Maintain cleanliness, hygiene, and sterilization of tools and work areas
  • Understand client requirements and recommend appropriate services
  • Assist team members during peak hours
  • Ensure client comfort and satisfaction throughout the service

Requirements & Eligibility

  • Minimum 2–4 years experience as a Beautician / All-Rounder
  • Hands-on experience in facials, waxing, threading, and basic hair services
  • Pleasant personality with good communication skills
  • Willingness to learn new techniques and services
  • Ability to work flexible shifts
