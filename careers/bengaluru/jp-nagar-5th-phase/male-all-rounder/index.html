Job Summary

We are hiring a Male All-Rounder who is proficient in men’s grooming services and basic salon treatments. The ideal candidate should be customer-focused, technically skilled, and committed to maintaining service excellence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform men’s grooming services including haircuts, beard styling, shaving, and hair treatments
  • Assist with basic spa services and head massages
  • Maintain hygiene and cleanliness standards at all times
  • Understand client preferences and deliver personalized grooming services
  • Promote salon services and ensure repeat customer visits
  • Support team members and follow salon SOPs

Requirements & Eligibility

  • Minimum 1–3 years experience in men’s grooming or salon services
  • Strong skills in haircuts, beard styling, and basic treatments
  • Good interpersonal and customer service skills
  • Ability to work weekends and peak hours
  • Willingness to grow and learn advanced grooming techniques
