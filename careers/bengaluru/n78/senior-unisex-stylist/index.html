Job Summary

We are looking for an experienced Senior Unisex Stylist who is passionate about hair, styling, and client satisfaction. The ideal candidate should be confident in delivering advanced hair services for both men and women while maintaining Naturals’ service standards and hygiene protocols.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide advanced haircutting, styling, coloring, and chemical services for men and women
  • Consult with clients to understand styling needs and recommend suitable treatments
  • Maintain high standards of hygiene, cleanliness, and workstation organization
  • Upsell salon services and retail products ethically
  • Mentor junior stylists and support team training
  • Ensure excellent customer experience and repeat visits

Requirements & Eligibility

  • Minimum 3–5 years experience as a Unisex Stylist in a professional salon
  • Strong expertise in haircuts, coloring, styling, and treatments
  • Good communication and customer-handling skills
  • Ability to work flexible shifts, including weekends
  • Certification in hairdressing or cosmetology preferred
