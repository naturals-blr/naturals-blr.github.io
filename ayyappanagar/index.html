Our Store

A premium experience
in Ayyappa Nagar.

Welcome to Naturals Unisex Salon Ayappa Nagar | Devasandra — east Bangalore's trusted beauty destination, conveniently located on Devasandra Main Road, KR Puram, opposite the landmark Lulu's Bakery in Basavanapura. Our Devasandra salon serves the rapidly growing communities of KR Puram, Mahadevapura, ITPL Road, Hoodi and Varthur with a full range of professional beauty services — from everyday haircuts and hair colour to bridal packages, deep conditioning treatments, skin care and body wellness therapies. Our team of experienced stylists knows that every client is unique, which is why each visit begins with a personalised consultation to understand your hair type, skin concerns and lifestyle. Families in KR Puram have trusted Naturals for premium-quality beauty services at honest, transparent prices. Whether you're looking for a weekend blow-dry, a pre-festival makeover, or a full bridal preparation, our Devasandra team is here to deliver results that last. Part of India's most respected salon network, Naturals brings 25 years of national expertise to your neighbourhood. Book your appointment online or walk in at Maruthi Tower, 1st Floor. Welcome to our brand new, state-of-the-art luxury location in Devasandra. We bring international styling standards and elite skin care to the KR Puram community.
