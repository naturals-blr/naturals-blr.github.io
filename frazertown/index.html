Our Store

A premium experience
in Frazer Town.

Welcome to Naturals Unisex Salon Frazer Town — centrally located on Mosque Road, Pulikeshi Nagar, above Poorvika Mobile Store, in the heart of one of Bangalore's most vibrant neighborhoods. Our Frazer Town salon caters to a diverse clientele from the CBD, Richards Town, Lingarajapuram, and Ulsoor, combining classic elegance with modern hair and skin innovations. Our team specializes in precision styling, global highlights, keratin treatments, and full bridal packages for all communities, with deep experience adapting treatments to diverse hair textures and skin tones. Experience premium grooming and elite styling at heritage Bangalore’s top beauty space on Mosque Road.
