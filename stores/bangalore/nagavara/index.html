Our Store

A premium experience
in Nagavara.

Welcome to Naturals Unisex Salon at Nagavara — north Bangalore's most convenient premium salon, located on the 1st Floor of , above to Paakashala Restaurant Nagavara, just minutes from Manyata Tech Park and Hebbal. Designed for the busy professional, our Elements Mall salon offers express and full-service beauty treatments that fit your schedule — from quick blowouts and express facials to full hair colour sessions and relaxing spa therapies. Whether you're a tech professional stepping away from the office, or a weekend shopper looking to unwind, our certified stylists deliver precision results with premium products every time. Our services include haircuts and styling for all hair types, global and fashion hair colour, keratin and smoothening treatments, deep conditioning hair spas, advanced facials, and de-tan and clean-up treatments perfect for Bangalore's sun and pollution. As part of India's most trusted salon network — with 25 years of expertise — Naturals brings world-class beauty services to your neighbourhood mall. Book online or visit us on the 3rd Floor at Elements Mall. Located inside the premier Elements Mall, our salon offers high-end grooming and beauty solutions tailored for the sophisticated urban lifestyle.
