Our Store

A premium experience
in JP Nagar 5th Phase.

Welcome to Naturals Unisex Salon JP Nagar 5th Phase — south Bangalore's premium beauty destination on 100 Ft Ring Road. Nestled in the heart of JP Nagar's 5th Phase, our salon has been the go-to choice for residents of JP Nagar, Banashankari, Jayanagar and Uttarahalli for expert hair care, radiant skin treatments and flawless bridal packages. Our team of certified stylists specialises in precision haircuts, balayage and global colour, keratin smoothening, hair spa therapies, and a full range of facials tailored to Bangalore's climate and skin types. Whether you're preparing for a wedding, a corporate event, or simply your weekly self-care ritual, every visit at our JP Nagar salon is crafted around you. Part of India's most trusted salon network with over 25 years of expertise across 1,000+ outlets, Naturals brings national-standard beauty services to your neighbourhood — with transparent pricing, premium products, and no surprises. Book your appointment online or walk in at Anand Onyx, 1st Floor, 15th Cross, 100 Ft Ring Road. Discover a sanctuary of beauty in South Bangalore. Our JP Nagar 5th Phase stylists specialize in global hair trends and rejuvenating skin therapies.
