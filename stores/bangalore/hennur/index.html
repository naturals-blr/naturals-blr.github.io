Our Store

A premium experience
in Hennur.

Welcome to Naturals Unisex Salon Hennur — north Bangalore's trusted beauty salon, conveniently located above SBI Bank on Hennur Main Road at Hennur Cross, serving the fast-growing communities of Hennur, Kalyan Nagar, HBR Layout, Horamavu and Banaswadi. Hennur has transformed into one of Bangalore's most dynamic residential hubs, and our salon has grown with it — offering the neighbourhood access to premium salon services that were once only available in central Bangalore. From expert haircuts and the latest hair colour techniques, to relaxing hair spa therapies, brightening facials, de-tan treatments and full bridal packages, our certified team delivers outstanding results at honest, transparent prices. Our Hennur team is passionate about making every client feel pampered and confident — whether you're a working professional looking for a quick style refresh, a new bride preparing for your big day, or a family visiting together for the weekend. As part of India's most trusted salon chain with 25+ years of experience, Naturals brings the gold standard in beauty to your doorstep. Book online or walk in at Hennur Main Road. Dedicated to excellence, our Hennur team provides personalized beauty consultations and advanced treatments designed for the modern resident of North Bangalore.
