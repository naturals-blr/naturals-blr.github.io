Our Store

A premium experience
in Frazer Town.

Welcome to Naturals Unisex Salon Frazer Town — centrally located on Mosque Road, Pulikeshi Nagar, above Poorvika Mobile Store, in the heart of one of Bangalore's most vibrant and well-connected neighbourhoods. Our Frazer Town salon caters to Bangalore's most diverse clientele — from professionals working in the CBD and Shivajinagar, to residents of Frazer Town, Richards Town, Lingarajapuram and Ulsoor. We offer a comprehensive range of beauty services including precision haircuts, global and highlight hair colour, keratin and rebonding treatments, hair spa, advanced facials, bridal and party makeup, mehendi and full bridal packages for all communities. What sets our Frazer Town salon apart is our team's deep experience with diverse hair textures and skin tones — from fine straight hair to thick curly and wavy types, and from fair to deep skin tones. Every treatment is selected and adapted to give you the best possible result. Transparent pricing, premium products, and a warm, welcoming space — that's the Naturals promise. Visit us on Mosque Road or book your appointment online. Nestled in the heart of heritage Bangalore, our Frazer Town salon combines classic elegance with modern hair and skin innovations for an elite experience.
